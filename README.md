DimmerApp is a small Windows app made mainly for laptop users who feel like the lowest brightness setting still isn’t low enough.
It lets you dim your screen below the system minimum by adding a smooth overlay, which is especially helpful at night or in dark rooms.

It also includes:
-An option to start automatically with Windows
-Adjustable intensity levels below the system limit
-A simple scheduler to turn dimming on and off at specific times

No complicated setup.
No heavy background processes.
Just a simple way to make your screen easier on your eyes.

This is a personal project I built for myself and decided to share. If something doesn’t work perfectly on your system, I’m sorry in advance, but feel free to open an issue and I’ll try to improve it.
If you’ve ever opened your laptop in the dark and felt personally attacked by the brightness, this is for you.
